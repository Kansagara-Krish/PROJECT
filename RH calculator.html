Room Harmonic Calculator


NOTE:Nodes are the point is 0 (Zero) Amplitude and Antinodes are the point Maximum Amplitude of the wavelength

Node: This is a point where the air does not experience much displacement. In terms of sound, it could feel quieter because the sound wave's amplitude is nearly zero at that point. It's less likely that you’ll experience the full loudness or richness of the sound.

At the Node: If you're seated exactly at the node, you'll experience the least sound intensity. This is generally not ideal for listening because you’ll miss out on the full dynamic range of the sound.

Antinode: You feel higher sound pressure near this spot as well. So the sound experience is mixed, as both a node and antinode occur at the same point due to the alternating nature of the standing wave.

Antinode Region: Generally, sitting closer to an antinodal position (not directly at the node) will give you better sound experience, as you will experience maximum sound displacement, meaning louder and fuller sound.
